Our Team

  • David Ammon

    Managing Partner



Two days after the terrorist attack that we know as 9-11, he received a call from the Department of Defense. What came from that phone call changed everything.

After arriving onsite at the Pentagon, I began evaluating and assessing the damage created by the 9-11 terrorist attack. For the next three years, we worked tirelessly to restore the largest, most recognizable office building in the country. For our efforts, we received a "Letter of Commendation" from our US Congress.

From a contracting perspective, our resume includes multiple national projects for the Department of Defense, Department of the Air Force, Navy and Army, NASA, General Motors, Ford Motor Company and multiple NFL, MLB and NBA stadiums and arenas. We all know the importance of schedules, completion dates and bottom line budgets. Throughout the years, we have watched in horror as work place injuries went virtually unnoticed and satisfied. These were all hard working people that had placed themselves and their bodies on the line each and every day for the sake of the schedule and the client. Injuries do to accidents being what they are, tragically cut short the professional and financial lives of our hard working American citizens.

Therefore, we decided to close our contracting company and open American Disability Solutions. We found that it was time to work for you, the hard working people of our glorious country. As important as schedules and bottom line costs are, the human body and spirit must be placed high above and beyond all else. When it comes to getting you your Social Security and Disability benefits, we remain highly focused and driven to meet and/or exceed your financial expectations.

Understanding the needs of you, our client, and comprehending the individuals expectations is where it all begins. Having experienced most workplace injuries, we will be your best advocate when it comes to retrieving your benefits for you. We will be by your side until our task is complete and you are 100% satisfied. As a benefit to you, we will not charge you ANY out of pocket fees or expenses. We are here to work solely for you.
