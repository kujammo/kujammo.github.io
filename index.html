Who WE ARE

American Disability Solutions is America's premier Social Security and Disability institution.

Mission

Our mission is to give a voice to the millions of Americans who are disabled and unable to work, getting them the Social Security Disability benefits to which they are so richly entitled. We will never stop working for our clients, even in the face of adversity.

Team

Our team is highly focused on helping clients navigate the complex procedures and hearings necessary to obtain their disability benefits. We understand the financial and emotional burden that the Social Security Disability Administration process can put on our clients. As a result, we assist our clients with all aspects of the process, from filing initial applications to representing them at hearings (if necessary).

Customer Support

Lastly, we are devoted to pursuing our clients interest. At all times, we go out of our way to be available to our clients. The team at American Disability Solutions works to serve the disabled, to promote social justice and to provide economic security to our clients. At American Disability Solutions, our team is dedicated to advancing our clients interest and committed to the pursuit of excellence.
